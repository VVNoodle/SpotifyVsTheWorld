FAQ

What does this app do?

You know how Twitch and YouTube Livestreams have realtime viewer counts? This is like that but for Spotify. When you're listening to a track on Spotify, this app shows the number of users also listening to the same artist of that track at the same moment.

Is this only for Mac?

Yes. At least for now. We have plans to include more platforms

How do you count tracks with multiple artists?

One of the artists is randomly chosen. Yeah. This doesn't make sense and of course the plan is to include all of them. A reminder that It's not 1.0 yet :)

How do you count remixes?

The artist being remixed gets the count. This is clearly not ideal. The plan is to consider both the artist(s) being remixed AND the remixer(s)
